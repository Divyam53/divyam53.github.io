The $360K Migration: Redis to Dragonfly

Infra Optimization DragonflyDB Cost Control

The Challenge: Scaling our ranking infrastructure was becoming cost-prohibitive. We needed higher IOPS without exponentially increasing the cloud bill.

Read Migration Strategy

Strategy: I led the evaluation of Dragonfly as a multi-threaded alternative to Redis. The migration required a zero-downtime shadow-write approach to validate data parity before the final switch.

  • Metric: Achieved 3M+ IOPS.
  • Result: Reduced caching infrastructure costs by ~91%.
